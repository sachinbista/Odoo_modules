POS SIGNATURE

This module helps to capture the signature of the customer at the time of placing any order. It allows customer to draw a signature in point of sale screen which not only displayed on the receipt but also saved in the back.

This Extension Allows

  • Saves and displays the signature of your customer(s).
  • Displays the signature of your customer(s) in POS Ticket.
  • The signature of the customer(s) is also saved in Odoo backend.
  • Ease of usage.
  • Just install and Use.
