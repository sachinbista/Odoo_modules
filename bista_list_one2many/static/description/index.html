Disable a past dates in Date Picker widget 

                
    e.g.
    <field name="validity_date" options="{'datepicker': {'minDate': 'now'}}"/>
There are three option for minDate ('yesterday', 'now' and 'tomorrow'). It will work on date and datetime fields.
